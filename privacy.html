Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 2025

We value your privacy. This application uses the TikTok API only to allow you to upload videos to your own TikTok account. We do not collect, store, or share any personal information outside of what is required to authenticate with TikTok. Authentication tokens are stored securely within your own n8n instance. No data is shared with third parties. You may revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings.
